The 300 block of Waverly Avenue has seen the most parking tickets Jan 1, 2023 - March 1st, 2024.

This horizontal bar chart is presenting data on parking tickets issued in various locations throughout Syracuse, NY from January 1, 2023, to March 1, 2024. Each bar represents a location, and the length of the bar correlates to the number of tickets given. The top location is 300 Waverly Avenue, with the highest number of tickets at 2,040.
